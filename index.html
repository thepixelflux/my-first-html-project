My Favourite City DELHI

Delhi: A Living Mosaic

Delhi is not just a city — it’s an experience. It brings together people from every corner of the country, each adding their own flavor to its dynamic culture. The city speaks in many languages, wears many traditions, and thrives on its contrasts. Every day in Delhi is a mix of old values and modern rhythms, of quiet resilience and loud celebrations. It’s a place where identities blend but voices remain distinct. In its chaos lies a strange sense of order — that’s the spirit of Delhi.

Major Tourist Attractions in Delhi

Here’s a list of the prominent tourist attractions in Delhi, covering historical landmarks, spiritual sites, and modern attractions:

  • Red Fort – Iconic Mughal fortress and UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • India Gate – War memorial and popular evening spot
  • Lotus Temple – Baháʼí House of Worship known for its flower-like shape
  • Lodhi Garden – Lush garden with tombs and jogging paths
  • Connaught Place – Colonial-era shopping hub with modern flair
  • National Museum – Extensive collection of Indian art and history
